After choosing a graduate program and selecting a college, finding a job that files for H-1B Visa Petitions has always been a subject of interest for international students, and skilled laborers from other countries who plan to work in the US.
?
There are companies all over the US that file for H-1B petitions for their talents, but discovering those companies, and especially roles is still a mystery and work with just trial and error, which results in stress.
?
We can help you with narrowing down the possibilities to land a better job and relieve your stress. This piece of visualization helps all aspiring candidates to make an informed decision on the location, company, roles, and domains. This poster incorporates 3 Million applications from 2011 to 2016 and focuses mainly on Certified applications.
?
We used R to clean the dataset and make the visualizations. We used Adobe Illustrator to create a poster representing all our information. 
?
In our first chart, we took our data on the basis of their case status. On top, we have the certified applications which are the approved H1B. Since we only want our applications to be approved all our analyses are based on certified applications. 
?
Then we looked at the location where with the most number of H1B applications. As we can see from the line chart which shows the H1B application trend for the top 4 most states. We can see that California has seen a constant increase in the number of applications. Whereas NY has seen a decline in the number of application in recent years
?
To look into more depth about the locations for the most number of H1B. The darker the shade of green represents the number of applications. As we can see that California has a darker green shade on the west coast, whereas New York and New Jersey have a lighter shade of green. 
We then looked at the top 10 companies sponsoring the highest number of H1B visas. As we can see most applications are sponsored by International IT companies such as Infosys, Tata Consultancy Services. 
Going to the end of our analysis we look at the average salaries of the most common job titles for the approved H1B petitions. As we can see that the most common job titles are from the IT sectors. Such as Data Analyst, Data Scientist. 
From the exploration of the above questions, we know that:
1) California, New York, and Texas sponsor the highest number of H1B. Which are known for their strong IT Sector 
2) The number of H1B Sponsored for California has gone up each year, while in New York it has declined. 
2) From 2011 - 2016 foreign tech companies such as Infosys and Tata Consultancy Services have sponsored the most number of people. 
3) Most H1B filings are done for people working in the IT sector.
